Yaaw Professional STEM Sorority

The Yaaw Professional STEM Sorority is a community of young ladies in science and technology.

As a member of the Yaaw Professional STEM sorority you will benefit academic, leadership, and career advancement opportunities.

You will have access to microcourses that give you soft skills relevant to the job market.

The sisterhood brainstorming sessions will open up your mind to new possibilities and how you can solve various global challenges using technology.

STEM SISTERS

Members of the Yaaw professional STEM Sorority fondly refer to each other as STEM sisters.

That is always a reminder that we should treat each other with love in the team's engagements.
